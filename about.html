Our Mission

We coordinate advocacy campaigns and events to engage the student community in the global response to HIV and other diseases.


Dignitas International Youth Club (University of Toronto Chapter) seeks to provide a quality of life with dignity for children, youth and families affected by HIV/AIDS in resource-poor settings. We plan and execute a number of campus events such as seminars, coffeehouses, and fundraisers to promote HIV/AIDS-related issues among the university community.
